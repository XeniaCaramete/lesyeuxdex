about the eyes

I've always been fond of writing – it gives me a sense of self-awareness. To sit and observe the world might be a beautiful thing to do – but to sit, observe and capture it has always seemed to bring me one of the purest forms of joy.

And from one day, writing alone felt like missing a vast part of this ideal of mine of capturing the world natural, raw, and unbothered by my presence – so I got a camera. From getting my own camera, I remembered being a child and taking snapshots of my mom reading, my plushies sitting in an impressive yet questionable equilibrium on my desk, my friends attending my birthday party, and, however, anything that seemed precious to me back then.

Now I'm 21 and nothing changed that much from back then – I still write and take snapshots of beings, things, and places that I find precious. So les yeux de x is about everything I once admired but couldn't put into words – it is about opening my eyes to capture the world.
